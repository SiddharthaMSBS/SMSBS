Why Siddhartha Memorial?

With the view of providing value-based quality education for all round development of overall students, this school was established in the year 2048 B.S. as one of the pioneering organization in the field of education imparting distinct quality education in an easily accessible, tranquil and learning friendly environment for the children. We are commitedly focusing on imparting students to learn being self confident, self discipline and determined of achieving knowledge based practical education to make them able, decent and dignified citizens of the 21st century.
